Josie

Full Name

Josephine "Josie" Lloyd

Species

African Wild Dog

Theme Song

I Know Why (And So Do You) [Instrumental]

Many people consider Josephine to be the reserved and analytical type, at least on the surface.

She is a highly guarded and independent woman. Her strong sense of responsiblity and attention to detail has made her an ideal wife in the eyes of many, though her neuroticism, anger issues, and crass nature just as quickly drive them off.

Trivia

  • Despite being legally classified as an African Wild Dog on her paperwork, she has enough fox ancestry in her bloodline to be mistaken for an African Wild Dog/Fox mix.
