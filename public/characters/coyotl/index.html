They have been spotted, time and time again and throughout history, as nothing more than a footnote. The local people have called them by many names, but only Cóyotl seems to stick.

They are a force to be reckoned with. A sharpshooter, a gunslinger, an outlaw, a savage, a murderer, and many more titles have been flung at them. She wanders the desert and its many isolated towns to pick up newcomers to the West to join them on an aimless journey.

Their newfound companions never last long in their prescence.

Trivia

  • They tend to shapeshift frequently, but their etheral eyes remain the same with each new form.
  • Their characterization is heavily based on Judge Holden's violent tendencies - minus the more disgusting stuff he's done. They serve as a counterexample to the sanitized image of cowboys, and are a symbol of the violence cowboys wrecked on America via Manifest Destiny.
